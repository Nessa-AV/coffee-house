Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes, we accept reservations for parties of 6 or more. For smaller groups, we operate on a first-come, first-served basis.

Do you offer catering services?

Absolutely! We provide full catering services for events, meetings, and special occasions. Contact us at least 48 hours in advance.

Are you hiring?

We're always looking for passionate coffee lovers! Send your resume to careers@coffeehouse.com.

Do you have WiFi and power outlets?

Yes, we offer free high-speed WiFi and plenty of power outlets for our customers.
